City MUN

JECRC MUN is immensely proud to announce its collaboration with CityMUN.
CityMUN brings together students from across cities to have the Model United Nations.
They have teamed up to organize an enriching Model UN experience for both the novice and
advanced delegates, offering them the chance to develop important skills like public speaking,
conflict resolution, consensus building and countless others, while also promoting a more
in-depth understanding of the world around them.
It provides a platform to the budding leader to have discussion over global issues like climate change,
women's rights violation, terrorism, human rights violation etc.
