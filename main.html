MESSAGE FROM THE SECRETARY GENERAL


- Dhruv Laddha       Secretary General

Greetings Delegates
Since the time I've been involved in the MUN circuit, I've realized that over the past few years the concept of debating is lost in thin air and the orientation has shifted from what the UN actually stands for.
Dressed up in formals when you hold a country's placard, you're not just a single entity anymore, you're a delegate, a representative of a country speaking on the behalf of all it's citizens. You embody their spirit, you mirror their personality and let it all show in the words you speak, that is the essence of the United Nations.
We here at JECRC strive to provide you with that experience and the aura of pure debating along the lines of the United Nations.
It is an honor and a moment of pride to be the Secretary General of the seventh edition of JECRC MODEL UNITED NATIONS and an even greater pleasure to be a part of the legacy it holds.
This year we'll be simulating 5 committees and deliberations between delegates from all across the country and on behalf of my entire team I extend a very warm welcome to you all for JECRC MUN 2018.

Dhruv Laddha
Secretary General, JECRC MUN 2018
